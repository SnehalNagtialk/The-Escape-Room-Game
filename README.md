The Last Room – Developed a logic-based escape game where players must explore the environment, collect items 
in an interactive inventory system, and solve puzzles to unlock the final door. Designed engaging puzzle mechanics 
that require players to discover and combine items to progress. Implemented hidden clues and environmental 
storytelling to enhance immersion. Focused on user-friendly UI/UX design for intuitive gameplay. Optimized game 
performance and interactions for a smooth player experience.
